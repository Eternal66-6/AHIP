07 – AHIP Demo and Pitch Guide

AHIP – AI Healthcare Intelligence Platform

Demo script and product pitch guidance.

Real Rails Agentic AI Internship Project | Enhancement Pack

Demo Objective

The demo should prove that AHIP can convert fragmented healthcare workflow data into coordinated agentic recommendations.

Recommended 10-Minute Demo Flow

  1. Open Executive Dashboard and show high-risk healthcare workflow cases.
  2. Move to Operations Dashboard and show risk queues.
  3. Open a claim delay case.
  4. Show patient, claim, provider contract and benefit context.
  5. Show agent timeline: Claims Review Agent, Provider Contract Agent, Compliance Agent, Escalation Agent.
  6. Open recommendation detail and explain evidence.
  7. Accept or override recommendation with audit trail.
  8. Show compliance/audit history.
  9. Close with SaaS roadmap.

Pitch Summary

AHIP is an Agentic Healthcare Operations Intelligence Platform that helps healthcare teams detect workflow gaps, coordinate claims/contract/benefit/compliance intelligence and prioritize actions using auditable multi-agent AI.

Do Not Say

  • Do not say AHIP diagnoses patients.
  • Do not say AHIP approves claims automatically.
  • Do not say AHIP is production-compliant healthcare software.
  • Do not say AI decisions are final.

Say This Instead

  • AHIP supports healthcare operations intelligence.
  • AHIP provides explainable recommendations.
  • AHIP keeps humans in the loop.
  • AHIP is designed for workflow coordination and auditability.
