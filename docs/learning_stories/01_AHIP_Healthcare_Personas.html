Purpose

This document defines the main users AHIP is designed for. Personas help interns understand who uses the system, what they care about and what decisions the product should support.

PersonaRolePain PointsAHIP ValueKey Screens
Claims AnalystReviews pended, denied or exception claimsManual review, unclear reasons, missing contextGets claim risk summary, missing data and recommended next actionClaims Risk Queue, Case Detail, Agent Timeline
Provider Contract AnalystReviews provider contract and network configurationContract mismatch, effective-date issues, provider mapping confusionGets contract alignment alerts and evidenceProvider Issue Queue, Contract Detail, Recommendation Detail
Benefit Configuration AnalystHandles benefit plan rules and operational provisionsBenefit mismatch, authorization rules, coverage ambiguityGets benefit validation findings and flagged casesBenefit Validation Queue, Case Detail
Healthcare Operations ManagerManages team workload and escalationsNo single view of bottlenecks, delayed cases, ownership gapsGets prioritized operational dashboard and escalation queueOperations Dashboard, Escalation Board
Compliance ReviewerChecks documentation, audit trail and governanceMissing evidence, poor traceability, manual audit effortGets compliance alerts, audit trail and override historyCompliance Dashboard, Audit Logs
Executive ViewerLooks at overall operational healthNeeds concise metrics, not detailed case handlingGets executive summary, trends and high-risk areasExecutive Dashboard

Persona-Based Design Rule

Every dashboard or feature should answer: who is using this, what decision do they need to make and what action should they take next?
